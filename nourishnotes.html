Meal Prep

Meal prepping is a game-changer for college students. I've made so many good meal prepped meals this year.

My favorite is my Mediterranean bowl.

5 Minute Medeterranean Bowl Recipe

Ingredients for 2:

  • 2 cups cooked baked chicken, diced or sliced (from meal prep)
  • 4 cups fresh arugula
  • 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling
  • Fresh lemon wedges

Instructions

If you prefer warm chicken, reheat your meal-prepped chicken in the microwave for 1-2 minutes or in a skillet over medium heat for 3-4 minutes.

  1. Divide arugula between two bowls as the base.
  2. Top with your meal-prepped chicken.
  3. Arrange tomatoes and cucumber around the bowl.
  4. Add a generous scoop of hummus to the side.
  5. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over everything.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and squeeze fresh lemon juice on top.

This comes together in just 5 minutes with your prepped chicken!
