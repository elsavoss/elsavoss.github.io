About Me

Hi! My name is Elsa Voss, and I'm a sophomore at UW Madison studying Information Science and Economics. I created this website because I believe that every college student deserves to eat well, even on a tight budget and busy schedule.

I'm from Chicago, Illinois, where I worked at a Lifetime Fitness this summer. The community at lifetime encouraged and inspired me to prioritize my health. I started cooking for myself more, and built habits that I could bring to college with me this year.

My Cooking Philosophy

I believe that cooking should be simple and accessible, budget-friendly, and fun. You don't need fancy equipment or expensive ingredients to make delicious, nutritious meals.
