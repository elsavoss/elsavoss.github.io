My chia pudding!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 3/4 cup milk of choice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. In a bowl or jar, combine chia seeds, milk, vanilla extract.
  2. Whisk or stir vigorously for about 30 seconds to prevent clumping.
  3. Let sit for 5 minutes, then stir again to break up any clumps.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results. The chia seeds will absorb the liquid and create a pudding-like consistency.
  5. Give the chia pudding a good stir before serving.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  7. Top with sliced banana and fresh blueberries.

The basic ratio is 1 part chia seeds to 4 parts liquid. Adjust for thicker or thinner pudding.
