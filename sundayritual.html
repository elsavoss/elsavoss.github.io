Shop Smarter, Not Harder

Your Sunday shopping ritual sets you up for a successful week of healthy eating. Learning to shop strategically means you'll stretch your food budget further while still eating nutritious, delicious meals.

Steps for a successful shopping trip!

  1. Check your pantry, fridge, and freezer to see what you already have
  2. Plan your meals for the week based on your schedule
  3. Write a detailed shopping list organized by store section
  4. Check store apps and websites for weekly sales and digital coupons
  5. Set a realistic budget and commit to sticking to it
  6. Eat a meal or snack before shopping to avoid impulse purchases
